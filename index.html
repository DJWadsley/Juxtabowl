How to play

Overview

You will be given two quizbowl questions and a prompt asking which of them is greater in a certain characteristic. Click on the question that you think matches the prompt.

Tossups

You can be asked which tossup had a higher conversion percentage, which had a higher interrupt percentage (more negs), or which tossup had the later average (correct) buzz.

Bonuses

You can be asked which bonus had a higher overall PPB, or which bonus had the higher difficulty dropoff between two parts (easy and medium or medium and hard).

Bonus Parts

Finally, you can be asked which individual bonus part had a higher conversion rate. Comparisons will always be made medium-to-medium, etc. Have fun!
